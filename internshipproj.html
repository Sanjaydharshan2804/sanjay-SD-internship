Our Specials

Kulfi(malai)

Our Malai Kulfi is a creamy, indulgent treat with a velvety texture and a subtle sweetness. It captures the essence of traditional flavors in every rich, satisfying bite.

Kulfi(kesar)

Our Kesar Kulfi is a creamy, saffron-infused delight with a rich, aromatic flavor. Each bite offers a luxurious taste of tradition and elegance.

Kulfi(elaichi)

Our Elaichi Kulfi is a fragrant, cardamom-infused dessert that delights the senses. The smooth, creamy texture pairs perfectly with the warm, aromatic spice of elaichi, creating a truly comforting and flavorful experience.

Our Story

At our ice cream shop, we’re passionate about reimagining traditional flavors with a modern twist. Starting with a love for authentic Indian desserts, we’ve crafted a unique selection of kulfis that blend classic ingredients with innovative ideas. From the rich, creamy taste of Malai to the aromatic elegance of Kesar and Elaichi, each kulfi is a reflection of our commitment to quality and tradition. We believe in bringing joy through every bite, offering a taste of nostalgia while exploring new culinary horizons. Join us on this delicious journey as we continue to create and share the finest kulfi with you.
